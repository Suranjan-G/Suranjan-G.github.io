# One paragraph per bullet — each rendered as a <p> on the About page
about_trajectory:
  - I started research at the **Defence Research and Development Organization (DRDO)** as a Junior Research Fellow in 2014, working with statisticians and psychologists at DIPR on evaluation platforms. After an M.Tech in Computer Science from IEM Kolkata, I returned to DRDO as a Senior Research Fellow, delivering illumination normalization and thermal image colorization modules under grant 2535/DIPR-II/MD/CARS-01, and publishing across SCI journals and international conferences.
  - At **Trimble**, I moved into industrial computer vision — multi-spectral odometry, 3D point cloud registration, and XR-based digital twin registration (winning the global 2022 Trimble Hackathon out of 250 teams). At **Ola Electric**, I led vision and ML work for the Kruti pipeline and the Ola Future Factory automation stack — micron-level 3D deviation detection, 6DOF pick-and-place for dark warehouses, and battery-line quality inspection.
  - I currently work as a **Research Scientist at Nasiko**, focused on agentic AI research and development — routing frameworks for orchestration, observability and explainability layers for A2A compliance, and cost-bound token strategies for agent usage.
