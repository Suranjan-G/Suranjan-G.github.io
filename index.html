Suranjan Goswami

AI & Computer Vision Researcher · PhD, IIIT Allahabad

Research Scientist at Nasiko, working on Agentic AI orchestration, observability, and explainability. Previously Senior Research Engineer at Ola Electric (Kruti pipeline; 3D vision for factory automation) and Computer Vision Engineer at Trimble. My PhD focused on thermal image processing, colorization, and generative models.

Recent: A* publication at ICLR 2026 — IndicVisionBench: Benchmarking Cultural and Multilingual Understanding in VLMs — and an Information Fusion 2025 paper (IF: 14.7) on a diffusion-based network for thermal pedestrian colorization.
